MITRE ATT&CK Text Reports Classifier

About

This work was requested from the cybersecurity company SamaPartners during a summer internship.


The application is designed to predict MITRE ATT&CK IDs based on text descriptions from company reports. It leverages a powerful machine learning model trained to classify (multi-label) up to 191 different MITRE ATT&CK techniques.
